About Search Applicants

With the Intern Tool, you can specify specific criteria to find matching applicants. For example, you search for applicants by who recommended them. You can then save your search so you can easily search all applicants at a later time by using the same search criteria, without having to specify all the criteria again.

You can search for applications by using one or more of the following criteria:

§    Keywords

§    Date range the application was submitted

§    Application status

§    Microsoft contact

§    Location or group

Specify a new search

1.       Click Search Applicants.

2.       Under Search Criteria, specify the options you want, and then click Search.

Work Flow

There are many user roles to use SpringField. The functions of SpringField are described according to different roles respectively in following.

User Guide for Employee in University Relationship Group

UR employee is responsible of the relationship between MSRA and the universities in the word. They hold a lot of events in various universities every year. During the university events or activities such as Microsoft Clubs, UR employee always gets a lot of good applicans. So they want to refer the applicants to MSRA. Using SpringField, UR employee is able to record the applicants’ information efficiently. The steps are shown as following:

1)       Open the main page of SpringField: http://msra-spfield/springfield/

 

2)       Click the “UR Referral” link on the left of the page.

 

3)       Input the emails of the applicants, if there are a lot of applicants, separate them with semicolon.

 

4)       Click “Send Invitation” and the every applicant will receive a email automatically notifying that he is invited to register SpringField to submit his application, ant the email also contain a hyperlink for applicant to submit his/her personal information.

 

 

User Guide for Hiring Manager

If you are want to hire an intern to help you do some research topic, such as computer vision, you can use SpringField to search proper applicants. For your interested applicants, you can arrange interview, make decision and ask for your group manager’s approval all through SpringField. With Window authentication, you need not any system account for log on the system.

1. Screen Out Applicants

1) First, open your IE and open the page: http://msra-spfield/springfield/, you can see the general information about SpringField. Also, if you are using SpringField, there will be a My To-Do List which lists the information and work items you care.

 

2) Click the link pointed by the red arrow, you can see the information about all the applicants on the page. The applicants items marked with green background are the applicants referred by Intern Recruiter.  

 

 

3) On the top of the applicants’ information, there is a filter. There are a large number of applicants. You can set the key words to filter out the applicants you mostly care. You can set several key words or only one key word for the filter. After you select the filter keywords (Status, Major Category, Degree, Interested Area or Begin Date), click the “Apply Filter” button, you will see the filtered results under the filter.

 

 

 

4) Also, you can use the search module to search the applicants. This module will search the applicants’ resumes and papers by your input key words. Click the “Search Applicants” link on the top of the page, and you can see the following page.

 

 

5)  Input the search key words and click “search” button, the searched applicants results are shown under the search bar with rank. The applicant on the top best matches the search condition.

 

6) You can see the detailed information by click the name of the application. Then you can see the basic information, application related information, education background information etc., shown as following:

 

7)  Click the green arrow before each information item and you can see more detailed information. The item will be unfolded and the green arrow turns white. Also, you can click the white arrow to fold the information table.

 

                  

 

8) Both on the applicants, search result page and the individual application information page, you can click “Add to Favorite” button to add the applicant to your favorite list. This is convenient for you to see the information when you visit SpringField next time.

 

 

9) You can see the applicant on you Interested Applicants list by click “Interested Applicants” link on the left of the page. You can see the detailed information about the applicant by click his name. If you want to remove some applicants, you can click the “Remove” icon at the right to remove individual applicants. You also can choose several applicants by clicking the check boxes at left and then click the “Remove Selections” at bottom to remove them together.

 

2. Arrange Interview

1)  Both on the applicants, search result page and the individual application information page, you can click “Schedule Interview” button to schedule an interview for this applicant.

 

                        　　　　　　　　　Applicants Page

 

                                    　　　　　　　　Search Page

 

                                     　　　　  Detailed Information Page

 

 

2)  Click the “Schedule Interview” button, you can see the following page to set the interviewer and due date about the interview. You can also see the applicant’s information from this page.

 

3) Fill the interviewers’ alias on the right text box and select the Due Date by clicking “select” button on the right.

 

 

4) Then click the “Schedule Interview” button. You will see a message box about confirmation. Click “Ok”, SpringField will automatically send email to the interviewers about this interview request. If you want to cancel this interview, click “Cancel”.

 

 

 

5) If there is some interview finish his interview and submit his feedback through SpringField, you will receive an email about “Interview Complete” as following:

 

 

6) Click the link in the email. You will see the page about the interview detail as following. You can also see the applicant’s information and the complete interview history and incomplete interview list.

 

7) From the complete interview history, you can see the detail about this interview about the complete time, the interviewer, his suggestion and detail comment.

8) For the incomplete interview, you can choose to wait for him to finish or change interviewer.

3. Ask For Group Manager’s Approval

1) Once you decide to hire or reject this applicant, you can make decision at the table “Hiring Manager Approval”. Select your suggestion hire of reject at right. If you want to reject this applicant, just select “Reject” and click “Approval”, the applicant will be sent to be directly rejected. Otherwise, select “Hire”. Then you can fill your comment in the textbox. You can select your group manager.

 

2) Click the “Approve” button, you will see a message box. Click “OK” to confirm it, SpringField will send email to your group manager about the detailed information and ask for his approval. Click “Cancel” to cancel.

 

 

3) Finish the interview process, and return to the homepage of SpringField.

 

 

4. Off-Line Hiring

Sometimes, you visit a university and some good students. You decide to hire a student during your visit. You don’t need to go through the whole interview process on SpringField. There is a Green Express for you to hire applicant off-line.

 

1) First, open your IE and open the page: http://msra-spfield/springfield/, you can see the general information about SpringField. Also, if you are using SpringField, there will be a To-Do List which lists the information and work items you care.

2) Click “Off-line Hiring” link, you can see a new page.

 

 

3) Fill the items list on the page about this applicant. Then select you group manager and fill you comment.

 

4) Click “Submit Request”.  You will see a message box. Click “OK” to confirm it, SpringField will send email to your group manager about the detailed information and ask for his approval. Click “Cancel” to cancel.

                                     

5) Complete and return to the homepage of Spring Field.

2) You can click the link in the email to see the detail information including the application’s detail information and the interview information and the hiring manager’s comment.
